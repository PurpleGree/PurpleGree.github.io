ABOUT

My journey into this fascinating world began with a spark of inspiration from hacking movies, leading me to the discovery of the Scratch programming language in 2019. The simplicity and power of drag-and-drop coding opened my eyes to the endless possibilities in software development.

Driven by curiosity, I dove deeper and embraced the elegance of Python, guided by the wisdom of “Automate the Boring Stuff with Python.” My commitment to mastering the craft led me to the Zuri Bootcamp in 2021, where I got introduced to backend development using Python and the Django framework. Although an unexpected setback caused a pause in my learning path, my determination remained unshaken.

In 2023, I returned to academia, enrolling in the Computer Engineering program to continue my quest for knowledge and achieve my dream in the tech industry.

Beyond the screen, I express my creativity through music, playing the guitar and piano, and producing beats with FL Studio. A lover of anime, I find joy in the vibrant stories and characters that fuel my imagination.
